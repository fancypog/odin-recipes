Brown sugar bubble tea

Ingredients

For the brown sugar syrup

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Optional: a splash of water to adjust consistency

For the bubble tea

  • 1/2 cup black tapioca pearls (boba)
  • 1 cup black tea, brewed and cooled
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Prepare Tapioca Pearls

    Cook the black tapioca pearls according to the package instructions. Usually, it involves boiling them and then letting them sit to achieve a chewy texture.

  2. Step 2: Make Brown Sugar Syrup

    In a small saucepan, combine brown sugar and water. Heat over medium until the sugar dissolves, stirring occasionally.

    Simmer the mixture until it thickens into a syrup. Adjust the consistency with a splash of water if needed. Let it cool.

  3. Step 3: Prepare Black Tea

    Brew a strong cup of black tea and allow it to cool. You can use tea bags or loose-leaf tea, depending on your preference.

  4. Step 4: Assemble the Bubble Tea

    Add a generous spoonful of the brown sugar syrup to the bottom of your glass.

    Pour the brewed black tea over the syrup, leaving room for milk and ice.

    Add the cooked tapioca pearls to the glass.

  5. Step 5: Add Milk

    Pour in the milk of your choice over the tea and pearls. Stir gently to combine.

  6. Step 6: Optional: Add Ice

    If you prefer a colder drink, add ice cubes to the glass.

  7. Step 7: Serve and Enjoy

    Use a long spoon or a wide straw to mix and enjoy your homemade Brown Sugar Bubble Tea!
