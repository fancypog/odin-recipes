Rice pudding

Description

Rice pudding is a classic dessert featuring rice cooked in sweetened milk, resulting in a creamy and comforting treat. Often flavored with vanilla or cinnamon, this versatile dish is enjoyed warm or chilled, making it a timeless favorite across different culinary traditions.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla or cinnamon (for flavor)
  • Optional: additional garnishes like raisins or nutmeg

Steps

  1. Step 1: Combine Ingredients

    In a pot, mix rice, milk, and sugar.

  2. Step 2: Simmer

    Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally.

  3. Step 3: Flavor

    Add vanilla or cinnamon for extra flavor. Adjust sweetness to taste.

  4. Step 4: Cook Rice

    Allow the rice to absorb the liquid and cook until it reaches a creamy consistency.

  5. Step 5: Serve

    Once the rice is tender and the pudding is creamy, remove from heat. Serve warm or chilled, optionally garnished with raisins or a sprinkle of nutmeg.
